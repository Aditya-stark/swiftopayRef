Cancellation Policy

Swifto Pay, a brand of Kanak Drishti Infotech Pvt Ltd, is a payment orchestration platform that facilitates online payments for merchants. We do not handle shipping or order dispatch, and any queries related to the cancellation of orders, refunds, or returns should be directed to the merchant from whom you made the purchase.

1 Failed Transactions and Refunds

In cases of a failed transaction where an order is not generated at the time of the transaction on the merchant's website or a refund is initiated, the customer should receive the credit in their bank account within 5-7 business days. If the customer has not received the amount, they may contact the merchant for further details, as refunds are initiated from the merchant's end.

2 Disputes and Resolution

If the merchant declines to process the refund to the customer for cases where services/products were not delivered by the merchant, the customer may contact Swifto Pay for resolution. We will work with the payment gateway partner via which the transaction was performed to resolve the issue.
